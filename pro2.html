Biography

Omer Albashier was born in a small town and discovered his love for boxing at an early age. With relentless hard work and passion, he rose to prominence and became one of the most celebrated boxers in history.

Achievements

  • World Boxing Champion - 2020
  • Olympic Gold Medalist - 2018
  • National Boxing Champion - 2015-2019
